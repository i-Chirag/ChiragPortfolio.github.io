The Person Behind the Dashboard

Hi, I’m Chirag – a data analyst with a passion for turning raw data into meaningful insights. With a strong foundation in Statistics, Machine Learnig, Python, SQL, and data visualization, I specialize in discovering patterns, solving business problems, and communicating insights clearly.

I completed my Master’s in Data Science and Analytics from Chandigarh University after earning a Bachelor’s in Computer Application from Guru Nanak Dev University College, where I first developed my fascination with data and how it drives the world around us. During this time, I also earned a 7 band score in IELTS, sharpening my global communication skills and opening doors to international perspectives.

Since 2019, I’ve been building stories through numbers—not just for clients, but also through my own creative pursuits like YouTube content creation, scripting, and storytelling. Whether it's analyzing social media engagement, predicting trends, or optimizing strategies, I believe in the power of data to drive smarter decisions. Through Analytics by Chirag, I aim to showcase not just my projects, but the analytical thinking and curiosity behind them. Every chart, model, or dashboard here represents a challenge solved, a pattern discovered, or a decision made smarter.
